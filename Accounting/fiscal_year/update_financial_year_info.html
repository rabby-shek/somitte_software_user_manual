update financial year info

First you need to successfully log in to the software: - To update the financial year data, follow these steps: -

Step-1
From Accounting, click on the financial Year section.

Step-2
After clicking on the financial year section, a new list will appear. From there, click on the updated financial year information section. Step 3 After clicking on the section all the money years will be seen. Click the Edit button to update all the information for the specific year. Step 4 If you want to close the financial year, click the close button. Step 5 After clicking the Edit button, a form will appear. Fill out the form and click the Update button. Step 6 If you want to delete all the information in the form, click the Cancel button.
