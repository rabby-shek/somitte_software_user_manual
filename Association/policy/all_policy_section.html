Follow the steps below to view all policy list: -

Step-1
First select the policy option from the association.

A new list will appear after clicking on the policy option.

Step-2
Click the All Policy option from the list.

All the policy list can be seen after clicking on All Policy option.

Step-3
Click the show button to view information about a specific member from the list. After clicking the show button, all the information of the specified member will be displayed.
