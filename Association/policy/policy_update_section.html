Follow the steps below to update the policy information:-

Step-1
First select the policy option from the association.

A new list will appear after clicking on the policy option.

Step-2
Click the Update Policy Info option from the list.

After clicking on the Update Policy Info option, a list of all the account holders can be seen.

Step-3
Click the Edit button to update a specific member's information.

Step-4
Click the Delete button to delete the policy.

Step-5
After clicking the Edit button, a form will appear. Fill in the form correctly.

Then click the Update button.

step-6
If you want to delete all the information given in the form, click the Cancel button.
