All Scheme

Follow the steps below to view all the schemes: - You must first successfully log in to the software.

Step-1
Click on the Schemes section from the Association.

Step-2
After clicking on the Schemes section a new list will appear from there

Step-3
Click on the All Schemes section.

All the schemes will be displayed after clicking on All Schemes section.

Step-4
If you want to see the details of a particular member's scheme, click on the profile button.

After clicking on the profile button, the specific member scheme will be displayed.
