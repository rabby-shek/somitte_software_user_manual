Scheme Update Section

Follow the steps below to update the information in the scheme: -
First you need to successfully login to the software.

Step-1
Select the Schemes section from the Association.

A list will appear after clicking on the Schemes section.

Step-2
Select the Update Scheme Info option from the list.

All the schemes will be displayed after clicking on option.
Step-3
Click the Edit button to update the information on the specific scheme.

Step-4
If you want to delete the scheme, click the Delete button.

A form will appear after clicking the Edit button.
Fill out the form correctly.

Step-5
Click the Update button.

Step-6
If you want to undo all the information given in the form, click the Cancel button.
