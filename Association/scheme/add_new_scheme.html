Add New Scheme

Follow these steps to add a new scheme: -
You must first successfully log in to the software

Step-1
Click on the Schemes section from the Association.

Step-2
After clicking on the Schemes section a new list will appear from there

Step-3
Click on the New Scheme section.

Clicking on the section will bring up a form.

provide all the required information in the form accurately.

Step-4
When the information is finished, click the create button.

Step-5
If you want to delete all the information given in the form, click the Cancel button.
